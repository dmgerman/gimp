Layers, Channels and Paths Dialog

The Layers, Channels & Paths dialog is an auto switching dialog which means that the currently active image is is the one present in the tabfolders of Layers, Channels and Paths. You can turn off Auto and choose another opened image in the Image drop down menu.

Please see each tabfolders help page for help about Layers, Channels and Paths.

Additional Information

Default Keyboard Shortcut: Ctrl+L
