Undo History

Will show the history of your image editing along with thumbnails. This makes it very easy to jump back and forth in the command history. Tip: Always use Undo History when you work on hard and difficult tasks. It's a whole lot easier to jump ten steps back just by clicking in the Undo History dialog than doing it with Ctrl-Z.
